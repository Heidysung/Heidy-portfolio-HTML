ABOUT ME

MY BACKGROUND

I am a San Francisco Bay Area based Web / Visual Designer.

I have in AA in Graphic Design (2017) from College of San Mateo and I am presently pursuing a BA in Digital Media at San Jose State University to further enhance my UX and digital communications skills.

My life-long passion for making art and illustration, has led me into the realm of web and graphic design, as well as creating wonderful experiences for users.

WHAT I'M LOOKING FOR

I am seeking junior designer, internship or freelance opportunities in web and UX design.

I intend to find a position where my versatile creative and graphic design skills are utilized and challenged, so I can grow and reach my full potential while contributing to the growth and success of the organization.

This poster was created for the international Russian Avant-Garde exhibit. It's dedicated to El Lissitzky. The concept of this poster was inspired by his quote, his approach to design and unique visual style.

PORTRAIT of MR.BEAN

My goal was to create an image using type as an expressive visual element. Can you recognize this person? Yes, it's Mr. Bean. I like his funny personality and how he always makes us laugh in the movies. But in real life, he is very reserved, kind, and quiet. I am inspired by the social work that he does and that's why I created this portrait.

FUTURA TYPE POSTER

I got inspired by the Futura typeface because it looks clean, modern and simple. As a designer, I believe that less is more. I researched the history of the typeface, and I try to make the poster look like from the time period where Futura was created in 1927.

ECO POSTER

This ECO poster addresses the global environmental problems of today. The poster depicts a seabird with a stomach which is filled with rubbish, representing human carelessness and the unfortunate consequence.
